Managing Books

Follow these steps to manage books in the system:

  • Adding a Book: Navigate to the books page and click on the "Add Book" button. Fill out the form and submit it.
  • Editing a Book: On the books page, find the book you want to edit and click the "Edit" button. Make the necessary changes and submit the form.
  • Deleting a Book: On the books page, find the book you want to delete and click the "Delete" button. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  • Updating Stock: On the books page, find the book you want to update stock for and click the "Update Stock" button. Update the availability and submit the form.
