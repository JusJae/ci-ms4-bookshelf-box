Creating a Subscription

Welcome to our Bookshelf Box subscription service! Here's how it works:

  1. Create a subscription option by deciding whether you want a one-off box or a recurring subscription (monthly or every 3 months).
  2. Choose how many books you would like in your box (1, 2, or 3 books).
  3. Create your subscription and proceed to make the payment.

The price of your subscription is based on the category of books you choose and the number of books you select. You will also receive a discount depending on the type of subscription you choose.

Subscription Types

  • One-off Box: Pay once and receive your box.
  • Monthly Subscription: Receive a box every month with a discount.
  • Every 3 Months Subscription: Receive a box every 3 months with a larger discount.

Discounts

The discounts applied are as follows:

  • One-off Box: No discount.
  • Monthly Subscription: 10% discount.
  • Every 3 Months Subscription: 5% discount.
